Personality & care

Miso

Dog · Border Terrier · Female

This is what your sitter will see in the shared view.

Favourite food & treats

Edit

Royal Canin Maxi Adult, 350 g twice daily (morning + evening). Loves dried chicken strips as training treats. No rawhide — causes stomach upset.

Likes & temperament

Edit

Calm and affectionate. Loves belly rubs and long walks in the forest. Enjoys meeting other dogs on leash. Sleeps on the sofa — allowed.

Not friendly with

Edit

Small rodents (strong prey drive). Off-leash terriers — gets overstimulated. Keep leashed in dog parks if terriers are present.

Behaviour notes

Edit

Barks at the doorbell — settles after 30 seconds. Can be left alone for up to 4 hours. Crate-trained but prefers the sofa. Knows sit, stay, come, paw.

Walking & routines

Edit

Morning walk 30 min (around the block). Evening walk 45–60 min (Stadtpark preferred). Always on leash near roads. Drinks from a collapsible bowl on long walks.

Special requirements

+ Add

Meds with meals, grooming, anything non-obvious
